Personal Bio

Hello, I'm Adrian San Pedro

I'm a passionate Front-end web developer based in Angeles City, Philippines with a strong interest in building user-centric, performant web applications. I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology specializing in Web Development at Holy Angel University.

My journey into web development started when I built my first website in high school — and I've been hooked ever since. I enjoy the blend of creativity and logic that coding offers, and I'm always chasing that feeling of shipping something that genuinely solves a problem.

When I'm not coding, you can usually find me exploring new technologies, making new designs, or playing strategy games. I believe in continuous learning and pushing the boundaries of what's possible on the web.
